Discover Healing in Nature At Evergreen Wilderness Therapy Retreat, we believe in the transformative power of nature. Nestled in the heart of Washington State's pristine wilderness, our retreat offers a sanctuary where individuals can reconnect with themselves, others, and the natural world.

Our mission is to provide a supportive and nurturing environment where participants can embark on a journey of personal growth, healing, and self-discovery. We combine evidence-based therapeutic practices with the healing power of nature to create a unique and effective approach to mental health and wellness.

Adventure Therapy

Engage in exciting outdoor activities such as hiking, rock climbing, and canoeing. These adventures are designed to build confidence, resilience, and trust while promoting physical and emotional well-being.

Workshops and Retreats

We offer a variety of workshops and multi-day retreats focused on personal growth, wellness, and specific therapeutic themes. Led by experienced facilitators, these programs provide in-depth exploration and healing.

Mindfulness and meditation

Learn techniques to cultivate mindfulness and inner peace. Our guided meditation sessions, held in serene natural settings, help participants develop coping skills and enhance emotional regulation.
